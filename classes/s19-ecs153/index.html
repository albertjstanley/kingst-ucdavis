Overview

This class focuses on computer security. Our goal is to train you how to think like an attacker, learn from past mistakes, and understand the primitives available to modern system builders to defend the computer systems of today and tomorrow.

The prerequisite for this class is undergraduate operating systems.

Lectures

You are responsible for knowing about all announcements made in lecture. We will discuss expectations about the project, suggestions for how to succeed, and grading guidelines in class, and general class policy issues, so make sure you don't miss any lectures.

Textbooks

The optional textbook is "Computer Security: Art and Science" by Matt Bishop. We will also include supplemental reading material in the lecture notes and schedule.
