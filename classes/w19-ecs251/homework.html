Homework

During the first part of the quarter, we will have three homework assignments. These assignments are designed to reinforce the concepts around threads from a programmers perspective, use the C or C++ programming langauge, and make heavy use of the pthreads abstraction.

Late homework

In general, we don't accept late homework assignments. However, to provide flexibility for students, we will offer all students one late day that they can use for either homework 2 or homework 3. To use a late day simply email Sam and Art and let us know that you plan to use one, and then submit your assignment sometime within the 24 hour window after the due day.

You only have one late day to use during the quarter, and it's designed to provide you with flexibility in case you get sick, have a family emergency, or any other unexpected events. Use your late day wisely.
