Grading

Turning in work and late policy
Late reports will receive no credit and they are due on the day before the lecture where we discuss the material. For anything that you turn in, we will use Canvas.

Cheating policy
I have a no tolerance policy on cheating, which includes copying from friends and plagarism. If you get caught cheating, you will get an F in this class and I will turn you into the univerisity's academic integrity group.

Quizzes
I will give you full credit on your lowest quiz grade for the quarter. That way, if you need to miss a class and there is a quiz, you won't be penalized. If you take all of the quizzes, I will replace your lowest quiz score with full credit.

Leading a lecture
My goal is for all of you to get full credit for leading a lecture! Here's how I will break down the points for your grade when you lead a lecture:
  • (1/4 to total) -- Show up to the pre-lecture practice talk and have a talk that is ready to present. It's ok if your talk isn't polished, and in fact you can expect it to change a lot after you get feedback, but it does need to be complete and ready to present.
  • (1/4 of total) -- Pick meaningful optional reading. Should have 2-3 optional reading items and a few sentences about each that explains why you picked them.
  • (1/4 of total) -- The presentation itself (should be 5-10 minutes long) that goes over (1) background on the required reading, (2) a balanced view of the issue at hand, and (3) a summary of the optional reading and why you picked it.
  • (1/4 of total) -- In class discussion. Create good questions, make sure there is a lively discussion.
As a challenge to the entire class: come prepared to class so that you can discuss the topics in a lively debate. Since your grade depends on it, please do make sure that you come to class prepared to debate.

Final paper
Your final paper is worth 20% of your final grade, here is how I will break down the points:
  • (5 points) -- Explanation of technology
  • (5 points) -- Explanation of ethical issues with that technology
  • (5 points) -- Depth of research
  • (5 points) -- Organization, flow, grammar, style, etc.

Grade breakdown
Your grade will be composed of the following items:

Item Percent Description
Class participation10% You are expected to participate in all of the classroom discussions, and I am hoping that you will express your views. Your grade for participation is based on attendance to discussion section, where you will get 2% per discussion section for showing up, for a total of 6% of your total grade. We reserve the right to use the final 4% for other forms of discussion, but we will define this later in the quarter.
Quizzes and reports20% You will turn in two brief reports (around 1 page) on the reading material, due the day of class during which we discuss the material (2% each for 4% of your total grade) + eight in-class quizzes (not announced, worth 2% of your final grade each for a total of 16%).
Final paper35% Your final paper will be approximately 2000 words long using a format that I will specify. Your final paper consists of two milestones: Milestone 1 (5% of your total grade) and Milestone 2 (10% of your total grade) + a final report (20% of your total grade).
Presentations35% During the quarter, you will lead the discussion for 1 lecture (15% of your total grade), and you will present your final project to the class (20% of your total grade). Your final presentation will be at the end of the quarter and will summarize your final report.
